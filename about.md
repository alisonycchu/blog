Hi, I’m Olivia. 

I jot most things down, if not every dialogue and passing thought. From seemingly casual 1:1s, work interviews, and weekly minutes, to chats over sangria, coffee, or quick breaths (depending on the time of the day), calls with long-distance friends, and one-minute dentist appointments.

For the sake of my poor memory and anxiety to keep track, I leave traces in text, which becomes a piece of tangible memorabilia for life’s conversations that shape my mind. And yes, I am a rambler, shown in the  string of commas I deploy.

Instead of becoming a YouTuber (1), Instagrammer (2) or dare I say, LinkedIn-fluencer, as medium of my unsolicited advice, unremarkable experiences and annoying voice (3), I’ve decided quickly that none of big corp platforms served the aesthetics and purposes of accurately reflecting my stream of consciousness. This is not to mention with and without engagement, my anxiety levels shoot up by sight of numbers that judge performance due to the education system i grew up in. By persuasion of my bf, I shall now on refer to as “lower-case-D”, d for short, we renewed my domain, and became deities to this world. Elegantly but simply put, a digital dump where I am compelled to be more intentional and well-organized with the text and spaces I publish.
